The F-35's Unrivaled Capabilities

                                     Sukhoi Su-57
                        Speed. Power. Supremacy.

Su-57 Domination

The Sukhoi Su-57 is Russia's first stealth multirole fighter, developed under the PAK FA program to replace the MiG-29 and Su-27. Featuring stealth, supermaneuverability, and supercruise, it is designed for aerial combat and ground strikes. First flown in 2010, its development faced delays due to technical issues, with the first aircraft entering Russian service in December 2020. The Su-57 is also marketed for export

                                     Chengdu J-20
                                    Mighty Dragon.

J-20 Capabilities

The Chengdu J-20, also known as the Mighty Dragon, is China's advanced twin-engine stealth fighter developed for the PLAAF. Designed for air superiority and precision strikes, it has three variants, including an upgraded airframe with thrust-vectoring engines and a twin-seat version for aircraft teaming. Originating from the J-XX program, it first flew in 2011, debuted publicly in 2016, and entered service in 2017, making China the second country to deploy an operational stealth fighter.


Sources: Chengdu









                                     F-22 Raptor
                        Digital, Dominant, Ready

The Definition of Air Dominance

The Collier Award-winning F-22 Raptor first took flight on Sept. 7, 1997 in Marietta, Georgia, after six years of development. It has delivered on its promise to provide unprecedented air dominance. Today, the Raptor continues to provide our nation and allies air superiority and is a pathfinder to next-generation technologies.
